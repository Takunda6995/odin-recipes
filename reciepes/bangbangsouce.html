Bang Bang Souce

Description

This bang bang sauce is a sweet and spicy
mayonnaise-based sauce that is good on fried fish, chicken, fried potatoes — almost everything!

ingredience

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons sweet Thai chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Stir together mayonnaise, chili sauce, Sriracha, and rice vinegar in a small bowl until smooth.
